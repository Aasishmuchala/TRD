Nifty lot size for P&L: use 25 units/lot (Nifty standard lot).
P&L per trade = lots * actual_move_pts * lot_size
where actual_move_pts = actual_move_pct/100 * entry_close
On BUY: pnl = +actual_move_pts * lots * 25
On SELL: pnl = -actual_move_pts * lots * 25
On tier=="skip" or direction=="HOLD": pnl = 0.0
